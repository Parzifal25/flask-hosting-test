Our Origin Story

Born out of a late-night brainstorming session and a shared frustration with the enormous food wastage problem, Resavr was founded by a group of techies and changemakers who believed in using AI for good. From humble beginnings to making real-world impact, every meal saved is a small revolution.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between surplus and scarcity by connecting restaurants with food banks in real-time, ensuring no good food goes to waste.

Our Vision

We envision a future where food waste is history, hunger is optional, and every meal matters. A smarter, more sustainable food ecosystem for all.
